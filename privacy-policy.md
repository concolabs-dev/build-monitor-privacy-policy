# Privacy Policy — Build Monitor App

**Effective date:** 2026-01-12

This Privacy Policy explains how **Build Monitor App** (“we”, “our”, “app”) collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the app.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

---

## 1) What this app is for
Build Monitor App helps teams manage construction/site work such as attendance, daily reports, and related site records.

---

## 2) What information we collect

### A) Information you provide
We may collect:
- **Account details** (such as name, email, phone number, username)
- **Login information** (for signing in and account management)
- **Work/site information** you enter (daily reports, notes, tasks, progress updates)
- **Attendance information** you submit (check-in/check-out times, status)
- **Photos / attachments** you upload (for site reporting), if your app supports it
- **Messages or support requests** you send to us

### B) Information collected automatically
We may collect:
- **Device information** (device model, OS version, app version)
- **App activity logs** (basic events like crash reports and error logs)
- **Network info** (IP address, general connection info)

### C) Location information (only if your app uses it)
If the app requests location permission, we may collect:
- **Approximate location** or **precise location** to support features like attendance verification, site-based check-ins, or maps.

> If you do not allow location access, some features may not work (only if your app depends on it).

---

## 3) Why we collect this information (how we use it)
We use your information to:
- Create and manage user accounts
- Let you log in and use the app
- Record attendance and site activity
- Store and show daily reports and site records
- Improve app performance, fix bugs, and prevent crashes
- Protect the app from misuse and keep it secure
- Respond to your support requests

---

## 4) Do we share your information?
We do **not** sell your personal data.

We may share information only in these cases:

### A) With service providers (to run the app)
We may use trusted services for:
- Hosting servers / databases
- File storage (for images and documents)
- Error monitoring / crash reporting (if enabled)

These providers can only use data to provide their service to us.

### B) For legal reasons
We may share information if required by law, court order, or to protect users, our business, or public safety.

### C) Within your organization (if this is a company/team app)
If your account is created under a company/organization, some data (like attendance and reports) may be visible to authorized admins/managers in that organization.

---

## 5) Data retention (how long we keep data)
We keep your information only as long as needed to:
- provide the service,
- meet legal/accounting needs, and
- resolve issues.

If your organization manages your account, retention may follow your organization’s policy.

No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep your information safe.
